Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our Aseel roosters and breeding program

What makes Aseel roosters different from regular chickens? +

Aseel roosters are a specific breed of gamefowl known for their strength, stamina, and fighting spirit. They have a more muscular build, stronger bones, and greater intelligence compared to regular chickens. They are bred specifically for their game qualities and have been selectively bred for centuries for these traits.

How do you ensure the quality of your birds? +

We maintain strict breeding records and only breed from champion bloodlines. Each bird receives proper nutrition, healthcare, and conditioning. We also conduct regular health checks and provide appropriate vaccinations. Our birds are raised in spacious, clean environments that promote their physical and mental well-being.

What age is best to purchase an Aseel rooster? +

The ideal age to purchase an Aseel rooster is between 8 to 12 months. At this age, their physical characteristics are well-developed, and they have received proper training. Younger birds (4-6 months) are also available for breeders who want to raise them with specific training methods. We advise against purchasing birds older than 18 months unless they are proven champions.

Do you provide delivery services for birds? +

Yes, we provide safe and secure delivery services across Pakistan. We use specialized transport containers that ensure the birds' comfort and safety during transit. For international customers, we can arrange export documentation and transportation through approved channels. Delivery charges vary based on location and number of birds.

Can I visit your farm to see the birds? +

Absolutely! We encourage serious buyers to visit our farm and see our birds in person. This allows you to observe their condition, temperament, and quality firsthand. Please contact us in advance to schedule a visit so we can ensure someone is available to give you a proper tour and answer all your questions.
